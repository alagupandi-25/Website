Pioneer Technologies

1.Biomass Briquetting and Pelleting Technology

Biomass briquettes are a biofuel substitute to coal and charcoal, made from agricultural and forestry waste. This process converts low-density biomass into high-density biomass briquettes.

2.Activated Carbon Generation

Generation of activated carbon through continuous carbonization of wood species, used for various industrial purposes.

3.Wood Value Addition Technology

Skill development in wood value addition, such as antiques and carvings, utilizing CNC router machines.

4.Oil Extraction from Tree Borne Oil Seeds

Extraction of oil from tree-borne oil seeds, which have various domestic and industrial uses, including agriculture, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and bio-diesel production.

5.Steam Distillation for Essential Oils

Extraction of essential oils from tree components using the steam distillation method, offering business opportunities in both local and international markets.

6.Mini Clonal Propagation Technology

Production of quality planting material and its mass multiplication through mini clonal propagation technology, aimed at commercial tree-based nurseries.
